# Parlay Odds Formula Explained With Examples

Parlay math is straightforward once every leg is in decimal format.

## Step 1: Convert Each Leg to Decimal

If your book shows American odds, convert each leg first.

## Step 2: Multiply Decimal Legs

- Combined Decimal = Leg1 * Leg2 * Leg3 ...

Example:

- 1.91 * 1.80 * 2.10 = 7.22

## Step 3: Calculate Payout and Profit

- Payout = Stake * Combined Decimal
- Profit = Payout - Stake

For $100 stake at 7.22 decimal:

- Payout = $722
- Profit = $622

## Step 4: Get Implied Probability

- Probability = (1 / Combined Decimal) * 100

For 7.22 decimal:

- 13.85%
