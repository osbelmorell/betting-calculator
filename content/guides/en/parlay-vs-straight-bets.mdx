# Parlay vs Straight Bets: Risk and Return Comparison

Both bet types can be useful, but they behave very differently.

## Straight Bets

- One outcome per ticket
- Higher hit rate
- Lower variance
- Easier bankroll control

## Parlays

- Multiple outcomes on one ticket
- Much lower hit rate
- Higher variance
- Bigger top-end payouts

## Practical Rule

Use straight bets as your core strategy and parlays as selective high-upside positions.

## Example

If you like three picks, compare:

1. Three straight bets at standard pricing
2. One three-leg parlay
