American odds can look confusing at first, but conversion is simple once you separate plus odds and minus odds.

## Formula for Positive American Odds

Use this formula:

- Decimal = (American / 100) + 1

Example for +150:

- Decimal = (150 / 100) + 1 = 2.50

## Formula for Negative American Odds

Use this formula:

- Decimal = (100 / |American|) + 1

Example for -110:

- Decimal = (100 / 110) + 1 = 1.909

## Convert Decimal to Fractional

After you have decimal odds:

- Fractional = Decimal - 1

Example for 2.50 decimal:

- Fractional = 1.50 = 3/2
